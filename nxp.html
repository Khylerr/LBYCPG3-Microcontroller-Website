NXP Semiconductors

NXP Semiconductors N.V. is a Dutch semiconductor manufacturing and design company with headquarters in Eindhoven, Netherlands. It is the third largest European semiconductor company by market capitalization as of 2024. The company employs approximately 34,000 people in more than 30 countries and reported revenues of $12.61 billion in 2024.

i.MX RT Crossover MCUs
  • The i.MX RT series bridges the gap between traditional microcontrollers and application processors by delivering application-processor-level performance, up to 1 GHz with the Cortex-M7 core, while retaining MCU simplicity like deterministic real-time response and no mandatory OS. These devices integrate rich peripherals including LCD controllers, camera interfaces, and high-speed connectivity.

S32 Automotive Platform
  • The S32 platform is NXP's scalable automotive MCU family designed for next-generation vehicle architectures, featuring Cortex-M (S32K series for body/zone control), Cortex-R (S32R for radar/ADAS), and Cortex-A (S32G for vehicle networking/gateways) cores, all sharing a common software development environment to streamline code reuse across domains.
